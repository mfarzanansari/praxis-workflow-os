# Roadmap

The roadmap describes investigation areas, not delivery promises. Evidence from real use determines priority.

## v1.1 candidates

- Public target-client eval reports and a repeatable review viewer.
- Stronger install verification across Claude Code, Codex, Cursor, OpenCode, and other `skills` CLI targets.
- More examples for physical, service, leadership, education, and accessibility-sensitive work.
- Profile-diff and blueprint-migration utilities.
- Optional event-log adapters with explicit privacy controls.
- Compatibility tests for the Prompt Polish handoff contract.

## Later exploration

- Signed profile and blueprint migrations.
- Pluggable runtime-memory adapters that remain separate from curated vault knowledge.
- A local visual interview companion for diagrams and workflow maps.
- Team and organization variants with role-aware permissions and shared governance.
- Reusable benchmark datasets for trigger precision, workflow completeness, and human correction rate.

## Explicit non-goals

- A giant catalog of role personas.
- Hidden autonomous actions or silent vault reorganization.
- Replacing every existing productivity system.
- Treating raw chat transcripts as durable memory.
- Bundling unreviewed third-party skills because they are popular.
